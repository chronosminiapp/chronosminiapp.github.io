Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Chronos

What is Chronos?
Chronos is a time-based mining app that runs as a Telegram Mini App. It allows users to earn CHRONO tokens simply by spending time in the app. While users mine tokens, the app collects anonymized data that helps train AI systems, creating a dual-purpose ecosystem.
How do I start mining?
Starting is simple: open Telegram, search for the Chronos Mining Bot, start the Mini App, connect your TON wallet, and tap "Start Mining." That's it! You'll immediately begin earning tokens based on the time you spend in the app.
What is the mining rate?
The base mining rate starts at 1 CHRONO token per hour. However, you can increase this rate by building streaks (consecutive days of mining), referring friends, and using Time Machine mods. With all multipliers active, you can earn up to 4.975x the base rate.
What data does Chronos collect?
Chronos collects anonymized interaction data such as app usage patterns, feature engagement, and session duration. This data is used to train AI systems for consumer behavior prediction. All data is anonymized and we never collect personal information without explicit consent.
When will CHRONO tokens be tradable?
CHRONO tokens will become tradable on TON-based decentralized exchanges during Phase 2 of our roadmap (Q3 2025). We'll announce the exact date and exchange listings closer to the launch.
How does the referral system work?
When you invite friends using your referral link, you receive 4 free hours of mining for each friend who joins. Additionally, you get a permanent 5% boost to your mining rate for each active referral. Your friends also get 2 free hours when they join through your link.
