GAMEPLAY

There's only one button: Start Mining.
When pressed, you can see stats as the app converts time into tokens.

It's a passive process that requires no action
other than running the game on your device.

Mining Modes

Boost your mining speed by unlocking advanced modes like Turbo, Hyper, and Ultra. These modes can multiply your mining rate up to 4x.

Daily Streaks

Maintain a daily mining streak to increase your rewards multiplier. The longer your streak, the more tokens you earn per minute of mining.

Passive Mining

Start mining and watch as your tokens accumulate over time. It's a passive process that requires no action other than starting the mining process.

Time-Based Rewards

The longer you mine, the more tokens you earn. Your mining time is converted into tokens with various multipliers based on your streak and mode.
