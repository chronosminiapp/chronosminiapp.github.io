Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Chronos mining and AI training

What is Chronos?
▼

Chronos is a time-based mining app on Telegram where users earn tokens by spending time in the app. Since 2023, we've been helping users earn rewards through our innovative streak-based multiplier system. While mining, user activity helps train our AI system that predicts consumer behavior on the TON blockchain.

How do I start mining?
▼

Simply open the Chronos Mini App in Telegram with a single tap - no registration required. You'll start mining automatically with free hours to get you started, and you can claim 4 free hours every 24 hours to maintain your streak.

How does the streak multiplier work?
▼

Each consecutive day you mine adds 4.975 to your multiplier. The formula is: 1 + (4.975 × streak days). This multiplier directly increases your mining rewards. The maximum multiplier is reached at 40 days, after which the price for buying hours starts decreasing.

How does my mining activity train the AI?
▼

While you mine, your anonymized activity data helps train our AI system to recognize patterns in user behavior across the TON blockchain. This includes engagement patterns, transaction timing, and feature preferences. All data is anonymized and aggregated to ensure your privacy while contributing to a system that will benefit the entire TON ecosystem.

How do leaderboards and competitions work?
▼

Chronos features multiple leaderboards that reset daily, weekly, and monthly. You can compete for top positions based on mining rate, streak length, referrals, and more. Top performers receive exclusive rewards, badges, and multiplier boosts. Special competitions with unique rules and prizes are also held regularly to keep gameplay fresh and exciting.

What are Time Machine mods?
▼

Time Machine mods accelerate how quickly you spend your mining hours. Turbo Mode (2x) spends 24 hours in just 12 hours of real time. Hyper Mode (3x) spends 24 hours in just 8 hours. Ultra Mode (4x) spends 24 hours in just 6 hours. Higher speeds mean faster mining but also quicker depletion of your hours.

How does the referral system work?
▼

When you invite friends to Chronos using your unique referral link, you earn 4 free mining hours for each friend who joins. You also receive a permanent mining rate boost for each active referral in your network. Our multi-tier referral system rewards you for your direct referrals and their subsequent referrals, creating a powerful network effect that benefits everyone.

What happens if I run out of hours?
▼

If you run out of hours, your mining will stop and you'll lose your streak. To prevent this, make sure to claim your free 4 hours every 24 hours or purchase additional hours to keep your streak going.

What are the AI agents mentioned in the roadmap?
▼

In Phase 3 of our roadmap, we'll introduce AI agents that can make calls, send emails, and manage messages on your behalf. These agents will learn from your communication style and preferences to represent you seamlessly. Chronos token holders will be able to train these agents using their tokens, creating a valuable utility for the Chronos ecosystem.

What can I do with Chronos tokens?
▼

Initially, Chronos tokens will be distributed through an airdrop to active miners. In Phase 2, tokens will gain utility across the TON ecosystem and provide access to exclusive brand discounts through our partnerships. In Phase 3, tokens will be used to train AI agents that can communicate on your behalf. Additional utilities include governance rights and access to premium features.

Do I need any blockchain knowledge to play?
▼

Absolutely not! Chronos is designed to be accessible to everyone, regardless of their blockchain experience. Our one-tap start process requires zero technical knowledge, and all the complex blockchain interactions happen seamlessly in the background. As you play, you'll naturally learn about blockchain concepts through our intuitive interface.
